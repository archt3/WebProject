Types

Here is a list of types of cars

Sports Cars:

Sports cars are light, nimble cars, often without a lot of horsepower.

Muscle Cars:

Muscle Cars are a largely American category of cars. They are heavy, with ample amounts of horsepower.

Supercars:

Supercars are often expensive, ranging from $100,000 to $700,000. They have more horsepower than sports cars.

Grand Tourers:

Grand tourers are a subset of supercars and are expensive cars, usually around $200,000. They are essentially more luxurious versions, like the Aston Martin shown.

Hypercars:

Hypercars are often extremely expensive, ranging from $700,000 into millions. They have more horsepower than supercars, but are also heavier, since they weigh more (a result of the additional weight of the engines, as well as the additional luxury features that owners demand).
